HOW TO INSTALL

  1. Enable the toggle above.
  2. Click "EXPORT SAVEFILE" on the General Tab.
  3. Open your device's file manager.
  4. Replace the contents of backup_savedata.ini with your new file.
  5. Open Retro Bowl > Save Slots > Tap "RETRO BOWL" logo 5 times to Restore Backup.
